About

Built for Perth drinkers, by Perth drinkers.

What Is PubCrawl Perth?

PubCrawl Perth exists because going out in Perth shouldn't require a research committee. We've walked every route, tried every stop, and put together crawls that actually hit good bars — no filler, no tourist traps.

The bar directory is growing. Every bar in here has been vetted by someone on our team or recommended by a local who knows their stuff. We're not Yelp — we're just making it easier to find a good night.

How We Pick Bars

We look for places with: good drinks, decent atmosphere, and no attitude. We care about free entry, good vibes, and whether a place is worth coming back to. We update the directory as Perth's bar scene changes — which it does, fast.

Responsible Crawling

Eat before you go. Pace yourself. Drink water between stops. Book ahead for rooftop bars on weekends. And if you're not Ubering home, you're doing it wrong.
